# Enterprise Support

## Overview

Aethel Enterprise Support provides expert assistance for mission-critical deployments. While community support is available for all users, enterprise support offers guaranteed response times, dedicated resources, and proactive guidance from DIOTEC 360's engineering team.

**Why Enterprise Support?**

Mission-critical financial systems cannot afford downtime or delays. Enterprise support ensures:
- **Rapid Issue Resolution**: Guaranteed response times for critical issues
- **Expert Guidance**: Direct access to Aethel architects and engineers
- **Proactive Monitoring**: We identify and resolve issues before they impact you
- **Custom Solutions**: Tailored development for your unique requirements
- **Risk Mitigation**: Reduce operational risk with dedicated support team

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I upgrade from Professional to Enterprise mid-contract?**  
A: Yes, we'll prorate your existing contract and upgrade immediately.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my consulting hours?**  
A: Additional hours available at $500/hour (Professional) or $400/hour (Enterprise).

**Q: Do you offer support in languages other than English?**  
A: Enterprise tier includes support in Spanish, Portuguese, French, German, Japanese, and Mandarin.

**Q: Can I get support for older Diotec360 versions?**  
A: Professional and Enterprise tiers include support for versions up to 2 years old.

**Q: What's included in "custom development"?**  
A: Features specific to your use case, integrations, optimizations, and tooling. Protocol changes require separate discussion.

**Q: Can I get on-site support?**  
A: Enterprise tier includes on-site support for critical incidents (travel costs additional).

**Q: What if I need more than 40 hours/quarter of custom development?**  
A: Additional development available at $400/hour or we can create a custom package.

**Q: Do you offer support for third-party Aethel tools?**  
A: We support the Core Diotec360 protocol. Third-party tools are best-effort.
